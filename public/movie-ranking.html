The Greatest Animes ever! According to Jay Hogan

My Top 3 Animes of All-Time.

Demon Slayer

This is my favorite anime because of the graphics and Tanjiro's determination is inspiring.

Naruto

I like Naruto because the story is crazy and the show builds emotional connection to characters.

Attack of Titan

The plot twist in the show keeps you interested. Very dramatic and suspenseful.
